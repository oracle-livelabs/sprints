# FastStart LiveLabs Workshops 

## Introduction  

Welcome to this **LiveLabs FastStart** workshop.

LiveLabs FastStart workshops give you clear, step-by-step instructions to help you quickly gain hands-on experience with the Oracle AI Database. You will go from beginner to confident user in a short time.

Each LiveLabs FastStart includes a video that shows you exactly how to complete the tasks.

We are committed to creating more LiveLabs FastStart workshops. You can find all available FastStart workshops in the FastStart Library in the menu to the left.
