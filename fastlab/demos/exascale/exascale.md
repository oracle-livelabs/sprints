# Introduction

## About Oracle Exascale Database Service

**Oracle Exascale** is the cloud-native evolution of Exadata, designed to let production databases start small, scale elastically, and tap into intelligent shared infrastructure. Built on Oracle AI Database and Exadata smart software, Exascale removes infrastructure management overhead while delivering the performance, availability, and security demanded by mission-critical workloads.

In this journey, you explore how Exascale:

- Launches with a single VM, 8 ECPUs, and 300 GB of Exascale Vault storage for a low-cost entry point
- Scales online to 10 VMs, 200 ECPUs per VM, and 100 TB of managed Exascale storage without downtime
- Pools compute and storage resources across tenants to maximize efficiency and resilience
- Uses hardware-accelerated RDMA over a 100G RoCE network for microsecond-latency data access
- Instantly delivers space-efficient database clones through redirect-on-write technology

### What You'll Discover:

- Why enterprises want Exadata-grade performance with cloud economics for smaller workloads
- How Exascale combines Exadata intelligence with automated cloud operations
- How direct RDMA connectivity and intelligent storage tiers unlock extreme throughput
- How thin clones empower development, testing, and recovery teams at scale
- How organizations such as HUS-yhtymä are transforming real-world healthcare workloads on Exascale

Exascale blends Exadata’s intelligent storage, SQL offload, and smart algorithms with cloud-native automation so teams can focus on databases, not infrastructure.

### Key Benefits:
