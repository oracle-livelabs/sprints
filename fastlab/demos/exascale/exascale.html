📋 What's Happening to Your Database

🚀 Starting Small

Your production database begins with minimal resources:

  • 1 VM running Oracle Database 26ai
  • 8 ECPUs for compute power
  • 300 GB of Exascale storage
  • Low entry cost with pay-per-use pricing

This is all you need to get started with Exadata-level performance.

📈 Scaling Your Database

As your workload grows, your database gains more resources online:

  • More ECPUs added dynamically (up to 200 per VM)
  • More Storage allocated on-demand (up to 100 TB)
  • More VMs join the cluster (up to 10 total)
  • No downtime during scaling
  • You only pay for what you use

Your database seamlessly grows with your business needs.

🏢 Your Database in Multi-Tenant Pools

Your production database sits within intelligent cloud pools:

  • Exascale Compute Cloud: Shared pool of powerful compute resources
  • Exascale Storage Cloud: Shared pool of intelligent storage
  • Triple-mirrored redundancy for your data
  • Oracle manages infrastructure, you manage your database
  • Other databases share the same pools (efficient multi-tenancy)

Your database benefits from massive shared infrastructure.

⚡ Direct RDMA Connection

Your database connects directly to storage with no intermediate tiers:

  • Direct IO path: Database → RDMA → Storage (no nodes in between)
  • Microseconds latency: Hardware RDMA implementation
  • Millions of IOPs/sec: Extreme throughput
  • 100G RoCE Network: High-speed connectivity
  • Data flows continuously between your DB and storage

This is what makes Exascale uniquely fast.

🔄 Your Database Spawns Clones

Your production database can instantly create thousands of clones:

  • Redirect-on-write: Clones share blocks with parent
  • Instant creation: No waiting, no copying
  • Space-efficient: Minimal storage overhead
  • Full performance: Native Exadata speed on clones
  • Empower every developer with their own database

Dev/test environments become effortless and cost-effective.
