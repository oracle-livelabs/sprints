# FastLab LiveLabs Workshops 

## Introduction  

Welcome to this **LiveLabs FastLab** workshop.

LiveLabs FastLab workshops give you clear, step-by-step instructions to help you quickly gain hands-on experience with the Oracle AI Database. You will go from beginner to confident user in a short time.

Each LiveLabs FastLab includes a video that shows you exactly how to complete the tasks.

We are committed to creating more LiveLabs FastLab workshops. You can find all available FastLab workshops in the FastLab Library in the menu to the left.
